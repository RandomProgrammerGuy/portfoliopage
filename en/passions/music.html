    Music

    With 9+ years of experience in music, it's hard to imagine my life without it! I started taking music courses at age 10 by joining Hoda Parvaneh's children's choir and music theory classes in my birth city of Shiraz, Iran. After roughly four years, I managed to join his live performance choir group, as well as the Hilda Recorder Ensemble, led by him. Around the same time, I started taking piano courses and transitioned to the piano as my main instrument. This interest for music made me eager to make my own origninal compositions, as well as transcriptions and covers. I have been feautured on my high school's talent show (2024) for my classical piano performance.
