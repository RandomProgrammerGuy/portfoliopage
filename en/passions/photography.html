    Photography

    Every day we see unique and baffling views. These are the sights I try to capture through photography. My interest for photography started appearing in 2017, originally being inspired by aesthetic photos I saw on Instagram. Not having a professional camera, I used my phone to take photos. As time went on, my work was noticed by my middle school in Iran, hence why I was chosen to be part of the media and photography team for the 2018 National Genetics Workshop hosted by our school. My photos have gained more than 2.6 million views on Unsplash and were featured in the UN Act for Nature collection and multiple Unsplash topics, as well as also being featured twice in my high school's talent show (2023, 2024).
