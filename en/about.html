    About Me

    I was born in Shiraz, Iran on May 28 2005. As I have grown up, my interests and passions have developed to form the person I am today and how I pass my free time. These passions include programming, design, music and photography. In school, math has long been my favorite topic, hence why I chose it as one of my two majors during my undergraduate studies. The other major I chose was CS, as I have also long loved computers and everything you can do with them. After my time in the US at age six, I developed outstanding English skills that I use in every day of my life at this point; and following my immigration to France in 2022, I have also become fluent in French. In 2024 I started learning German to enable myself to more easily explore and navigate Europe.
